Cracow

bike maps

One day I wanted to find an interesting cycling routes in my mobile phone. I didn't find that kind of app for my city - Cracow, so I created one on my own. Application contains of the best cycling routes and attractions on the road. Of course it is also integrated with GPS.

My app works on Android 2.2 and higher. You can download it from Google Play.

YaaP

App archives sms messages and call logs to cloud. You can sign in to account, mark "auto backup" option and forget about application or backup manually if You want to enjoy a nice user interface.

My app works on Android 2.1 and higher and it is only piece of the ecosystem. Development of server-side and desktop end-point is in progress, but it isn't my part of work.

I am coming

I have an amazing girlfriend. She loves to prepare delicious food for me when I come home from work. I was forgetting about sending a message to her that I am coming. This problem inspired me to create "I am coming" app. Now, my app works in the background and I don't have to worry. Application informs her about my current distance from home and the exact address.

App works on Android 2.1 and higher. If You want to test it now, do not hesitate to contact me. The app will also be available at Google Play soon.
